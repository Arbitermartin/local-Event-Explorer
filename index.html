About Us

Local Event Explorer - USA is a web application developed as a final project for the Web Front-End Development II course (WDD 330) by Martin Arexander Kasike. Our mission is to simplify the discovery of local events in USA, connecting event-goers with concerts, workshops, festivals, and more in their cities. By leveraging the Ticketmaster API, we provide a user-friendly platform to search events by city and category, view details, and save favorites for later. Inspired by a passion for community engagement, this project aims to make local experiences more accessible, even in their cities with limited event data, fostering stronger connections within USA communities.

Our mission is to simplify the discovery of local events in USA, connecting event-goers with concerts, workshops, festivals, and more in their cities.

Vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic, accessible web platform that empowers individuals across the United States to seamlessly explore and engage with local events, from vibrant concerts to enriching workshops and festivals. By harnessing the power of the Ticketmaster API, we aim to deliver a reliable, user-centric experience that not only simplifies event discovery but also fosters community connection and participation. We aspire to evolve this project into a comprehensive tool that adapts to diverse user needs, integrates additional event sources, and enhances interactivity, setting a foundation for innovative front-end solutions in community-driven technology.
